# Prototype Combat Reference

## Prototype 0.1 Goal

Two placeholders fight locally with movement, jump, basic attack, hitbox/hurtbox, damage, health bars, win condition, and restart.

## Current Combat Bias

- One basic attack per fighter first.
- Damage fixed before damage tables.
- Hitbox active for explicit frames/ticks.
- Hurtbox separate from body collision.
- Debug draw is part of gameplay development, not polish.
- Knockback is optional after hit detection works.
- Combos, specials, meters, cancels, blocking and throws are later unless reduced to a tiny experiment.

## Character Identity

Each character concept should include:

- technical concept;
- gameplay archetype;
- silhouette;
- placeholder representation;
- one or two moves;
- risk of scope.

Use `docs/templates/character-concept.md` for durable proposals.
