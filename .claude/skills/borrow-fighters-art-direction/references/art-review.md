# Art Review Reference

## Moods Under Exploration

- Terminal Arcade
- Compiler Lab
- Mascot Fight Club
- Office Surreal

No mood is final.

## Review Criteria

- Can the player identify the fighter in motion?
- Does the attack read before and during impact?
- Can the idea work with boxes, colors, and simple shapes first?
- Does it rely on copyrighted mascot likeness?
- Does it create asset workload before gameplay proves value?
- Does the palette keep foreground, hit effects, and UI readable?

## Asset Rules

- Mark placeholders clearly.
- Record source and license for external references.
- Do not commit final-looking copied art.
- Keep reference images in `assets/references/`.
- Keep temporary prototype assets in `assets/placeholder/`.
